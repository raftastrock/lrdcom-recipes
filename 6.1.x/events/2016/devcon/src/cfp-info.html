Program Policies

· By submitting a session for consideration, you authorize Liferay to promote, publish, display, and disseminate the content submitted to Liferay, including your name and likeness, for use associated with the Liferay Symposium North America 2016 conference.

· Speakers for accepted submissions will receive one (1) complimentary pass to the event for which their submission is accepted. This complimentary pass provides access to all conference sessions at that event.

· Acceptance of the complimentary pass must be in compliance with the policies of your employer, including conflict of interest, ethics, and gift policies. The complimentary pass will be canceled if the session is canceled or replaced.

· The complimentary pass is limited to one (1) pass per speaker and is not transferable.

· Liferay does not reimburse speakers for travel and expenses. Speakers are responsible for any applicable taxes, customs duties, or other expenses related to acceptance of the complimentary pass.

· Do not include proprietary or confidential material. The submitter is responsible for obtaining necessary permission to use all materials.

· Liferay reserves the right to edit session information for clarity, length, and style, and to ensure correct use of Liferay product names (for example, Liferay product names may not be truncated or abbreviated).

· All deadlines must be met. Any requests received after the deadline may not be approved, and any missed deadlines can result in cancellation of sessions.

· Liferay reserves the right to cancel a session or speaker if necessary.

· In accordance with Liferay's privacy policy, Liferay cannot release information on conference attendees.

 

Speaker Policies

· All accepted speakers will receive one (1) complimentary FULL PROGRAM registration for the Liferay Symposium North America 2016 event in Chicago, IL. All speakers must register themselves. Instructions on how to complete registration will be provided to accepted speakers.

Complimentary Speaker Passes

· Complimentary Speaker Passes are not transferrable.

· The complimentary speaker pass will allow access to the event in which you are presenting.

· If for any reason you need to cancel your speaking engagement, your complimentary pass will no longer be valid.

Housing & Travel

All speakers will be responsible for covering their own lodging and any related travel expenses. Housing information will be available during the registration process
