What Are We Looking For?

We want thought-provoking topics and useful lessons about how Liferay has met your business needs. If you have an interesting story (no sales pitches, please!), we want to hear from you!

Format

Total session time: 30-minutes. We recommend a 20-25 minute presentation with a 5-10 minute Q&A. All presentations must be delivered in English.

Categories

  • The Liferay Platform: We want to hear about all the interesting things you've done with Liferay. Here are a few ideas to help you get started: omnichannel, digital touchpoints, IoT, new features, best practices for Liferay architecture, backend and frontend infrastructure, mobility, developer tooling, identity management and administration, and support for extensions and integrations through the Liferay Marketplace.
  • Connected Technologies: These topics focus on mission critical applications used in conjunction with Liferay, such as support for dynamic languages and frameworks, DevOps tooling (Docker, Puppet), cloud deployments & infrastructure (Azure, AWS), security best practices, and dealing with data.
  • Vertical-Specific Solutions, Applications, and Use Cases: Since Liferay is applicable across industries, our attendees are interested to hear real-world applications in different verticals. Consider:
    • Financial services - retail banking, commercial banking, investment banking
    • Healthcare - patient engagement, insurance providers
    • Education
    • Government
